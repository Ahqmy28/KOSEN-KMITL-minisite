Curriculum Structure

Mechatronics Engineering

Mechatronics Engineering integrates knowledge from mechanics, electronics, and computer science, with a focus on robotics, to produce and develop smart technologies.

Focus on:

  • Design and development of robots and mechatronic systems
  • Design of electrical systems in robotics
  • Programming to control robot operation

Computer Engineering

Computer Engineering focuses on the design, development, application, and troubleshooting of all aspects of computer systems and operations, including AI, IoT, and Network and Cyber Security.

Focus on:

  • Computer design
  • Software programming
  • AI and IoT development
  • Network and network security

Electrical and Electronics Engineering

Electrical and Electronics Engineering integrates electrical engineering with electronics, applying and developing capabilities to master these two fundamental disciplines.

Focus on:

  • Electrical circuit design
  • Development of alternative energy
  • Electrical communication systems
